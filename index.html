Doughnut Clicker

Fred the Baker is tired of making real donuts and is ready to retire. He needs our help to keep his love of donuts alive. He wants you to create virtual donuts for the world to enjoy. It's always "Time to make the Donuts" and Fred is too busy doing that to give us guidance on how to be the "Best Donut Clicker on Earth".

This project was inspired by Cookie Clicker, a 2013 incremental game created by French programmer Julien "Orteil" Thiennot.
